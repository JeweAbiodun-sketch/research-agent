## Executive Summary
Anthropic, founded in 2021 by former OpenAI researchers Dario and Daniela Amodei, is a leading American AI company headquartered in San Francisco, California. Specializing in AI safety and research, Anthropic focuses on developing reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. The company's mission is to build AI systems that people can trust, while promoting safety and generating insights into AI's opportunities and risks. Anthropic's flagship product line, the Claude family of large language models, is designed with safety and interpretability at its core, catering to the growing demand for AI solutions in the enterprise sector. As of April 2026, Anthropic's annualized revenue run rate exceeded $30 billion, reflecting significant growth from approximately $9 billion at the end of 2025. In February 2026, the company secured a $30 billion valuation through a private funding round, highlighting its strong market position and potential for future expansion.
